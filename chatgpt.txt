topic_arn: ARN of the SNS topic to subscribe to. Usually from an aws_sns_topic resource.

protocol: The delivery method. Common options:

email, email-json

lambda

sqs

http / https

endpoint: The destination. For email it‚Äôs the email address, for SQS it‚Äôs the queue ARN, etc.

  runtime       = "nodejs18.x"

  name = "my-topic"
}

  topic_arn = aws_sns_topic.example.arn
  protocol  = "lambda"
